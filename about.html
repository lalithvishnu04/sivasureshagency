Our Story

From Erode to Across India

Founded in 2010, Siva Suresh Agency started as a small textile supply unit in Erode, Tamil Nadu — a city known for its rich heritage in textile manufacturing.

Over the past 15+ years, we have grown into one of the most trusted suppliers of hospital linen, medical uniforms, and hotel textiles. Our commitment to quality, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction has earned us partnerships with over 500 hospitals and institutions across India.

Led by our founders Mr. Siva and Mr. Suresh, we continue to innovate and expand our product range to meet the evolving needs of the healthcare and hospitality industries.

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality hospital linen and medical uniforms at competitive prices, ensuring comfort and professionalism for healthcare workers across India.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted and preferred supplier of institutional textiles, setting industry benchmarks in quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Quality first, customer commitment, timely delivery, continuous innovation, and ethical business practices drive everything we do.
